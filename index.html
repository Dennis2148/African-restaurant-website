About AfroDeli

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef , AfroDeli was born out of a passion to share the rich culinary traditions of Africa with the world. Chef Amina grew up in Senegal, where she learned traditional cooking techniques from her grandmother.

Today, AfroDeli brings together flavors from across the African continent - from the spicy stews of West Africa to the aromatic tagines of North Africa. We use authentic recipes passed down through generations and source our ingredients with care.
